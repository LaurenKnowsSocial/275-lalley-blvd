Enjoy this beautifully designed colonial on one of the most desirable streets in Fairfield's beach neighborhood — just two blocks from Penfield Beach, and a 10-minute stroll to town filled with great restaurants and the train to NYC. Unique personality flows throughout the home.

The first floor has a chef's kitchen with an island for everyone to gather; an oversized family room with double french doors that open to the fenced-in backyard; a large dining room that can sit up to 8; plus a half bath.

The second floor boasts a tranquil primary suite that overlooks the sunny yard, along with a spacious en-suite bath and steam shower. Two additional bedrooms, a full bath with tub, and a spacious laundry room complete the second floor.

A window-lined staircase leads to the third floor with a large bonus room, fourth bedroom, and beautiful marble full bathroom. Enjoy warm days on a spacious deck facing the heated saltwater pool. A separate pool house with powder room completes this backyard oasis.
